What It's Like to Work With Me

01

Discovery

Whether through call or email, send me all the details about your project. It can be as detailed or non-detailed as you want. I'll either write strictly based on your instructions or have free reign over the text.

Some send me outlines of the title, subheadings, bullet points, and keywords, while others simply tell me their topic and I figure out what to write. Either works for me.

02

Contract & Order Brief

Once we've got the project details out of the way, I'll whip up an agreement contract for the both of us to sign. This contract will include:

  • Your order brief with the final price
  • Hourly rate if we go beyond the revisions, words, and other terms of the agreement
  • Refunds and project termination conditions
  • Other terms relevant to the project

03

Down Payment

I require a 50% downpayment before each project.

Full payments before I start working are appreciated though!

04

First Draft

I'll send you a Google Doc link of the first draft, so you can easily highlight and make comments about changes you want made.

05

Revisions

I'll revise and polish the piece based on your comments.

06

Final Payment

When you're satisfied with the writing, I'll send the final invoice for the last half of the payment including the additional rates (if applicable).

For monthly retainers, I'll be sending an invoice once or twice a month.
